Masquerade and Games - 13 August 2011
Report by Peter Rolls

WOW! What a night! Our small soccer community sure know how to party! In Juan Antonio Samaranch's words: 'The Best Ever' North Ryde social function! A party long to remember and good to see the parents having more fun than their kids for once in a season!
  • Tony and Scott did a fantastic job as the band Shock Daze to get the 130 revelers up on their feet (not to mention Tony's huge efforts to get so many O35 party animals there!) Many revelers at the end left the golf club in a Daze (but this was no Shock!)
  • Margie's mammoth effort to not only compile the Trivia questions but also to check and tabulate the scores!
  • Lhan ensured all had a good seat and maintained the upbeat tempo through-out the night with her dancing. Sponsored by Red Bull!!
  • Mark was professional and smart to get the formalities and thankyou's over early so no interruptions to the final 2 hours dancing!
  • Paul 'Tony Barber Games King Host' executed the Trivia answers and Air Guitar contest with gusto! Air Guitar an hilarious highlight!
  • Great sponsors and prizes
WINNERS
  • Best Mask 1st Place: Sue Robinson (Under 7 White)
  • Best Mask 2nd Place: Marc van Hilten (Under 7 Green)
  • Trivia 1st Place: Wayne Fowler's O35 Div 6 Table (aka 'Trophy Hunters')
  • Trivia 2nd Place: Marc van Hiltens U7 Green Table (aka 'Bloody Awesome')
  • Best Air Guitar Female: Sheree
  • Best Air Guitar Male: Dave Anderson (Under 9 Div 1)
