Music Trivia Night - 7 August 2010
Report by Peter Rolls

FANGTASTIC NIGHT! (and they won the Best Dressed table prize!)

Party faithfuls rocked the Eastwood Rugby Club for our Music Trivia Night - the social event of the year! 76 adults plus 1 baby and I am sure Julia Gillard would have preferred to be at our event rather than the other masquerades she has had to endure!

Albert Einstein said: 'A life lived for others is a life worth living'. The Managers from the respective teams and the admin people of NRSC created an event and an atmosphere where others had a great time and in the process had a great time themselves.

Paul Uhrig and Lisa Gilbert set the tone of the evening with their eye-popping outfits (lucky that's all that popped out) and set the night on fire with Paul's choice of music and video clips, and Lisa (the tough Biker) who would give Todd Kenny a run for his money as a judge. Mark and Margie Simpson were the backbone of the night with superb Trivia questions, prizes and commentary. Lhan Gannon, Andrew Curdie and Bill Greer articulate and fun at announcing Trivia questions.

There were a wide variety of table themes and some just came as themselves but all participated in the spirit of the night and showed what this club is all about, having a fun 'red hot' go! A special mention to managers Joe Rosenfels (U14/3), Alex McCoy/Lhan Gannon (O35), Andrew Curdie and Shaune Houssenloge (U10/5) for organizing two tables each who coincidently took out most of the prizes.

A BIG Thank You to our sponsors who salivated at participating in this special event with tasty prizes: Callaghan Street Liquor Shop (Tony and Arlaine) - Wine to make you merry and good for the 'Jugular'! Luna Park passes for four provided by John Spencer - for more thrills and laughs to continue on from our hearty party! North Ryde RSL Club / Eastwood Rugby Club - Good rates on hiring the room and accommodating and friendly staff.


WINNERS

Music Trivia
  • 1st Place: Over 35's 'Easy Riders' with 143 points
  • 2nd Place: U14/3 'Fangtastic (Twilight with Taste, Leather & Lace)' with 141.5 points
  • 3rd Place: U14/3 'Arsenic and Lace' with 129.5 points
  • Last Place: Over 35's 'Way Over 35' with 110 points were awarded the 'Wooden Spoons'. Literally!
Best Dressed Individual
  • 1st Place: Split decision given to Andrew and Heather Curdie as Sandy and Danny from Grease
  • Special mention to Matthew Howard dressed as Neo from the Matrix. We could not give him a prize because we could not recognise him!!
Best Dressed Table
  • 1st Place: Fangtastic (Twilight with Taste, Leather & Lace). You would not want to be anemic around this mob!
  • Special mentions: Curdies table for Grease Is The Word - they even brought a real baby as a prop! And the awesome Admin table as the Village People set off the 'Singing Bee' challenge with an unrehearsed rousing rendition of YMCA!
Rock Goddess
  • Susan Zdilar from Easy Riders table with outstanding audience participation. Even Paul U had to leave his DJ post to join in with Susan's air guitar.
  • Special mentions to Ros Quinn (U6 Green 'Rhinestone Cowboys' table) and Francis Hesse ('Magnificent Seven' table) who threw everything at this challenge for their tables - Susie Quatro would be proud.
Rock God
  • David Lewis dressed immaculately as one of the Count Dracula's from the Fangtastic table and did air guitar recklessly!
  • Special mention to Adrian Kennedy who was exhausted after the prelim and still mustered enough energy for the final.
A final sincere thank you to Jillian Rolls for the many hours spent on emails and phone calls to get bottoms on seats! Much appreciated as this was the critical function to ensure patrons at the club get to experience this fun event.
