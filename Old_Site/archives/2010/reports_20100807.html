Match Report - 07 August 2010
On this page:  U14 
U14 Division 3
North Ryde 2  -  West Ryde Rovers 1  
After a week off, due to wet weather, the first round robin was played. As we finished 2nd in the competition we played 3rd place which was against West Ryde Rovers. Our team was down 2 players, Harry and Daniel were both sick.

We played well in the first half and Hayden scored, the result at half time was 1 - 0. The second half West Ryde Rovers regrouped and scored to level the game at 1 - 1. West Ryde momentum continued but we were just strong enough to stop them from scoring. Then with minutes to go Lewis, who Paul said was having a good day in goals, goal-kicked the ball over the half way mark, Daniel Tidke out-played and out-paced the backs to score! 

Final result 2-1. The parents heaved a sign of relief as we again won in the dying minutes. BTW, that was our 8th victory in a row. 

Well deserved player of the match was Chris Butterfield and the selection was made by Harry Chan who still attended the game to spur the boys on! I would like to thank all the parents who come to support our team, you do make a difference.

A win against Glenhaven next week will see us through to the finals.

- Joe Rosenfels (Manager)
