Match Report - 12 July 2008
On this page:  U10   U14 
U10 - Division 1
North Ryde 0  -  Putney Rangers 6  
Eight fifteen am at Bremner Park and only 4°C. Like the previous week the team had 4 players down. A big thanks is extended to Shannon Condon, Adam Nisbet and Thomas Hurst from U8 for helping out in such a big way for little guys.The three boys were major contributors to the performance of the team. It was noted many times during the game that the 3 boys out-shone some of the U10 players.

In the first half, NRS performed well, keeping Putney at 1-0. In the 2nd half Putney were awarded two free kicks, one from a hand ball and the other a back pass to the goal keeper. Both free kicks were saved by Josh. Josh did exceptionally well in goal. Ryan rose to the challenge in the goal in the second half. Ryan did a fantastic goal kick which landed at Frazer's feet just beyond the half way line.

Unfortunately, we did not manage to get the ball very far down the field from the half way line. Tom got his act together and was very much focused throughout the game with gaining and keeping possession of the ball. Tom was the much deserved player of the match for the 2nd week in a row.

- Lesley Campbell (Manager)
