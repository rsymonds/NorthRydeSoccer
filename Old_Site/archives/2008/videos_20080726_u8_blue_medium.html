The U8 Blue team is the first under-eight team to be graded and fielded by the North Ryde Soccer Club in the GHFA competition, and this young collection of talented and electrically-charged players have enjoyed a fair share of unprecedented success during their 2008 season. They have only lost one game to date, and even then it was on a day when they were unable to field a full-strength side.

Ever eager and willing, they are always early and present for their training sessions and Saturday morning matches, and are a force to be reckoned with when they take to the field in a bundle of unbridled energy and gusto. The video displays a collection of clips from their recent 10-0 win over the Macquarie Dragons who, although a weaker team, show that you can not let your guard down for one second when you're facing up to this young outfit of U8 Blue dynamos
