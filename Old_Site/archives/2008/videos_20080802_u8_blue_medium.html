The U8 Blue team delivered yet another terrific performance with a decisive 5 - 0 win against the Hills Hawks on Saturday the 2nd of August 2008. It was a game in which every Blues player gave it their all from start to finish, and the manner in which they manage their team-play and assemble drive after drive at goals is a treat for all their fans on the sideline 

It is also worth mentioning that three of the five goals scored came from extraordinary corner-kicks off Adam's boot that somehow found the back of the net without any further intervention from the Blues players. Magic.
