 ARCHIVES  >  2008  >  VIDEOS  >  13 JULY 2008 - WILDCATZ 
This is a sensational video clip of the now legendary Wildcatz in action. Steve Jacobson, team photographer extraordinaire and proud father of a Wildcatz player submitted this highlights package of their match against Epping YMCA on 13 July 2008, which the Wildcatz won by 5 terrific goals to none.

The video shows some of the quality attributes that have turned the Wildcatz into such a successful outfit, including speed, positional play, crossing the ball in front of the goalmouth, and that all-important ingredient called team work. The five goals were scored by four different girls and a very special first-goal to Ellen who chased the ball right up to the goalkeeper, forced an error and finished off with a fine shot at goal. And how about those celebrations at the end of the game?

It must be a girl thing
