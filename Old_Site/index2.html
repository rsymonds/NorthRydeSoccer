Welcome to the 2011 Football Season 
The North Ryde Soccer Club welcomes both new and returning players to the 2011 football season. Continuing on it's success in previous years, the club will field teams in both the Gladesville-Hornsby (GHFA) and North West Sydney Womens Football (NWSWF) associations.

The GHFA competition caters for mixed teams from U6 to O35 and matches are generally played on Saturdays. The NWSWF competition is for girls and women only and they play their matches on Sundays.
